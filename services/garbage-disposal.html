Fast Disposal Repair

Common Disposal Issues We Fix

  • 1. Humming but not grinding: Often a jam that can be safely cleared.
  • 2. Leaking under the sink: Seal or flange failure that needs professional attention.
  • 3. Not turning on: Electrical issue or motor reset required.
  • 4. Slow draining: Blockage in the disposal unit or connected drain line.
  • 5. Excessive noise: Internal hardware failure or foreign objects.

Upgrade to a Powerful New Unit

Today's garbage disposals are quieter and more powerful than ever. If your old unit is over 10 years old, it may be time for an upgrade to a stainless steel, high-torque model that can handle tough food waste with ease.

Our expert plumbers will recommend the right horsepower for your household size and ensure a vibration-free, leak-proof installation.
