Reliable Sewer Repair in Pittsburgh

Older clay and cast iron sewer lines are common in Pittsburgh and are highly susceptible to tree root intrusion and soil shifting. A failing sewer line is a serious health and property risk.

We use the latest diagnostic technology to find the exact cause of your sewer trouble, saving you time and money on unnecessary excavations.
