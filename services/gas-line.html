Working with gas lines requires specialized training and a commitment to safety. At B.E.T. Plumbing LLC, we are fully certified to handle all aspects of residential gas plumbing, from leak detection to new appliance installations.

Leak Detection

High-precision electronic testing to find and fix hazardous leaks.

Appliance Hookups

Safe connections for stoves, dryers, fireplaces, and pool heaters.

Smell gas? If you detect a "rotten egg" odor, leave your home immediately and call your gas utility company or 911. Once the area is cleared, call B.E.T. Plumbing for professional repair.
