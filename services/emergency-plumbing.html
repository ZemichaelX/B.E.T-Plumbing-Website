When Every Minute Counts

Plumbing emergencies don't follow a schedule. They happen at the worst possible times – late at night, on weekends, during holidays. When you're facing a plumbing crisis, you need help fast from a trusted professional.

B.E.T. Plumbing LLC provides 24/7 emergency plumbing service throughout Pittsburgh and surrounding areas. Our experienced team is ready to respond quickly to minimize damage, protect your property, and restore your plumbing system.

Common Plumbing Emergencies We Handle

Burst Pipes & Major Leaks

Frozen or damaged pipes can burst suddenly, causing extensive water damage. We provide immediate response to stop the water and repair the damage.

Sewer Backups

Sewage backing up into your home is a health hazard that demands immediate attention. We'll clear the blockage and sanitize the affected areas.

No Running Water

Complete loss of water service can bring your household to a halt. We diagnose and resolve issues preventing water flow to your home.

Water Heater Failures

No hot water in winter? Water heater leaking all over your basement? We provide emergency water heater repair and replacement.

Gas Line Leaks

Gas leaks are extremely dangerous. If you smell gas, evacuate immediately and call us. We'll dispatch a technician to secure the leak safely.

Overflowing Toilets

A toilet that won't stop overflowing can cause significant water damage. We'll stop the overflow and fix the underlying problem.
