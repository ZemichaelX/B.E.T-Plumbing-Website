General Questions

Do you offer 24/7 emergency plumbing services?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing service throughout Pittsburgh and surrounding areas. Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours, and neither do we. Whether it's a burst pipe at midnight or a sewer backup on Sunday, we're here to help. Call (412) 733-6355 anytime, day or night.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, B.E.T. Plumbing LLC is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We're also HomeAdvisor verified, giving you extra peace of mind that you're working with a trusted professional. All our plumbers are experienced and certified to perform plumbing work in Pennsylvania.

Do you provide upfront pricing?

Absolutely. We believe in transparency and honest pricing. Before we start any work, we'll provide a clear estimate so you know exactly what to expect. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges – just fair, straightforward pricing for quality work.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Pittsburgh, PA and surrounding neighborhoods including Mt. Washington, Squirrel Hill, Lawrenceville, Shadyside, Oakland, Brookline, Ingram, Crafton, Carnegie, Dormont, and more. Contact us to confirm we serve your specific area – we're always expanding our service area!

Service Questions

Do you work on older homes?

Yes! We have extensive experience working with Pittsburgh's historic older homes. We understand the unique plumbing challenges these properties present – from galvanized pipes to outdated fixtures – and have the expertise to handle them properly. Many of Pittsburgh's homes are 50, 75, or even 100+ years old, and we're well-versed in their quirks and systems.

What types of water heaters do you install?

We install and service all types of water heaters including traditional tank water heaters (gas and electric), tankless water heaters, hybrid water heaters, and high-efficiency models. We'll help you choose the right size and type for your home's needs and budget.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer preventive maintenance services to keep your plumbing system running smoothly and catch potential problems before they become expensive emergencies. Contact us to learn more about our maintenance programs.

Can you help with gas line installation?

Yes, we're certified to work on gas lines. We handle gas line installation, repair, leak detection, and appliance hookups. Safety is our top priority when working with gas systems.

Pricing & Payment

Do you charge for estimates?

For most jobs, we provide free estimates. For complex diagnostic work that requires significant time and equipment, there may be a service call fee which will be applied to the cost of the repair if you choose to proceed with us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards for your convenience. Payment is due upon completion of work.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with warranties on both labor and parts. Specific warranty terms vary by service and will be clearly explained before we start any work.

Emergency Situations

What should I do if I have a plumbing emergency?

First, try to minimize damage:

  • Shut off the main water valve if possible
  • Turn off the water heater for major leaks
  • Never try to fix a gas leak yourself – evacuate and call immediately
  • Move valuables away from water
  • Call us right away at (412) 733-6355

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We understand that minutes matter in a plumbing emergency. We strive to arrive within 60-90 minutes for most Pittsburgh area emergency calls, often faster depending on our current location and your proximity.
